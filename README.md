## About Me

I work at the intersection of **business, data, and AI**.

My focus is not on building models for the sake of building models.

I enjoy understanding how a business operates, identifying bottlenecks, inefficiencies, and opportunities, then designing AI-powered solutions that create measurable impact.

Depending on the problem, that solution might be:

- 🤖 AI Agents
- 🧠 Machine Learning Models
- 📊 Decision-Support Systems
- ⚙️ Automation Workflows
- 🏢 Custom Business Platforms
- 🔬 Fine-Tuned Models

Technology is a tool.

**Understanding the problem comes first.**

---

## How I Approach Problems

### 🏢 Understand the Business

Before writing code, I want to understand:

- How the company makes money
- What processes drive value
- Where time is being lost
- Which decisions are difficult
- What prevents growth

### 🎯 Find Leverage

Not every problem needs AI.

The goal is identifying the smallest intervention that creates the largest impact.

### 🚀 Build the Right Solution

Only then do I decide whether the answer is:

- Software
- Automation
- Analytics
- Machine Learning
- Generative AI
- Fine-Tuning
- Or a combination of all of them
